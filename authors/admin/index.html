Pieter Moris

Data scientist bioinformatics

Galapagos


I’m a biologist at heart with a passion for machine learning and statistical methodologies. After an education in both biology and statistics, I embarked upon my current journey as a PhD student in the field of bioinformatics at the Adrem Data Lab of the University of Antwerp and the biomina research network . There I leveraged machine learning and data mining techniques to study molecular host-pathogen interactions and the immune system. My current role is data scientist in the bioinformatics unit at Galapagos .

I strive to be a proficient data scientist and to apply my analytical and quantitative skill set in the context of biological and medical sciences.
